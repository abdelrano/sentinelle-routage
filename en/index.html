Routing breaks without warning

A hijack or a leak is silent and fast. RIPEstat will show it to you — if you happen to be looking at the right moment. Three documented cases:

Apr 2018 — MyEtherWallet

An Amazon Route 53 prefix hijacked (origin AS10297). Users redirected to a fake site, ~$150,000 in Ether stolen. The attack ran ~2 hours before anyone understood it.

Jun 2019 — Verizon

A route leak through a "BGP optimizer" propagated by Verizon. Cloudflare, AWS and thousands of sites unreachable for ~2 hours. None of the victims had done anything wrong.

Aug 2022 — Celer cBridge

An Amazon prefix hijacked for ~3 hours to redirect a crypto bridge to a malicious contract. ~$235,000 stolen before containment.

None of these incidents would have been stopped by a manual check run once a quarter. All were detectable within minutes by continuous BGP monitoring.

Frequently asked questions


How is this different from BGPalerter?

BGPalerter is an excellent open-source tool, but you have to host it, configure it, maintain it — and it produces no compliance reports. Sentinelle Routage is hosted, ready in 5 minutes, and generates the audit documents (NIS2, MANRS) that alerting tools don't.

How is this different from bgp.tools or Kentik?

bgp.tools is fantastic for exploratory observability. Kentik and ThousandEyes are enterprise platforms at 4-5 figures a month. Sentinelle Routage does one thing for human-scale networks: monitoring YOUR ASN plus the documentary evidence for your audits, at an engineer's price, not a CIO's.

Where does the data come from?

The data comes from RIPEstat (RIS), the RIPE NCC's public data service, RPKI validation included. No agent to install on your routers, no access to your network required.

Does NIS2 really require me to monitor my routing?

NIS2 doesn't mandate RPKI word for word. But the directive and implementing regulation 2024/2690 require risk-management measures and their documentation for the digital infrastructure sector. A monthly routing security report is exactly the kind of evidence auditors ask for. We help you have it without spending your nights on it.
